Welcome to my site!

This is the personal website of Animystic- a cosmic gumbo of disperate interests. The Universe has never arranged itself in this order ever before!

I would like to have the frame of this website ready and stable by the end of Summer. Here are my plans for pages:

  • About Me: Of course, a personal website should have information about the webmaster! And if I am trying to build a space where my diverse, niche, and disperate parts can fit together into a whole, then I got some 'splainin' to do.
  • Blog: I left Meta in January of 2025. I have no regrets, but I do feel cut off from a lifetime of relationships a bit. To combat that, I will post my life updates here, along with the aspirational essays, thinkpieces, and media reviews that will definitely happen and not remain just a fantasy.
  • Photos: I plan to share select photos here to document my life and share some creativity.
  • Shrines: Digital Shrines hold a special place in the history of the early Internet. They are pages or entire websites set up to share about special interests, including TV shows, video games, authors, gods, activities, etc. This is where I'm gonna go hard on the things I like and you can click through briefly and say "Wow. Everybody's so creative! This is not for me though."
  • The Cave: Got your gear? Protection charms active? Said your prayers? Then you might be ready to go spilunking into the depth of my psyche. This is an experimental offshoot for that which is occulted. Mind the Light.
